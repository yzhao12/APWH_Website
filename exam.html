Tips For Studying For The AP Exam

  • Start studying early
  • Buy the book Mr. Bruns tells you to buy
  • Do what's effective for you, whether it be watching videos or reading books
  • Take the practice tests in the study books
  • Prepare for all parts of the test, multiple choice and the three types of essays
  • Do practice essays, even though they're very painful, trust us, it'll pay off when you sit down with only two hours to write the real essays
  • Take Mr. Bruns's practice Exam, not the standard final, this is a no-brainer, the AP practice exam will prepare you for the real thing way better than the final can
