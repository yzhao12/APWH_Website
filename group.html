How To Organize Useful Study Groups

  • Don't multitask
  • Don't get distracted
  • Don't include those who won't take it seriously
  • Set a specific time
  • Prepare munchies so you won't get hungry
  • Don't study too late in the night
  • Pool your knowledge so everyone can learn what they don't know
  • Make sure youre group is a group of close friends, you'll get along better
  • Do some practice tests or take-home tests together
  • Be nice to each other, remember the Golden rule
  • If you can't meet in person, use a voice messaging service
