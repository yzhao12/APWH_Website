How To Read The Textbook Effectively

  • Don't read too fast
  • Always read with conprehension
  • Pay attention to pictures, graphs, and charts
  • Think about possible quiz questions
  • Specific dates don't matter as much
  • Remember the years of important events
  • You usually don't need to read the enrichment sections in the text
  • As with any class, pay attention to vocab or important words
